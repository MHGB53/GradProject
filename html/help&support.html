Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and following the instructions sent to your email.

How do I access my study materials?

All study materials are available through the "Smart Study Plan" feature on your dashboard. You can also access specific materials through the "Features" menu.

How do I track my progress?

Your progress is automatically tracked in the system. You can view detailed analytics on your dashboard and in the leaderboard section.
